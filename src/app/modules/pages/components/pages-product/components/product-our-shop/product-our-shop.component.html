A2 Gir Cow Milk

Pure Milk. Pure Wellness. Delivered Fresh Daily.

Our A2 Gir Cow Milk is sourced from naturally grazed, hormone-free indigenous Gir cows—a breed known for producing A2 beta-casein protein, which is easier to digest and offers superior nutritional value compared to regular milk. This makes it an excellent choice for growing children, the elderly, and even those with mild lactose sensitivity.

We follow ethical, cruelty-free farming practices and ensure that every drop of milk is untouched by chemicals, antibiotics, or artificial hormones. Collected fresh every morning and delivered directly to your doorstep in Pune, our milk retains its natural aroma, rich taste, and full-fat nutritional profile.
