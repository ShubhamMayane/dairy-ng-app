What is A2 milk, and how is it different from regular milk? {{ activeIndex === 1 ? '−' : '+' }}

A2 milk is milk that contains only the A2 beta-casein protein, unlike regular milk which often contains a mix of A1 and A2 proteins. A2 milk is easier to digest and may be gentler on the stomach, especially for people with milk sensitivities. It’s also believed to reduce bloating and discomfort commonly caused by A1 milk.
